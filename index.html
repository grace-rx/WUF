Our Mission

We United Foundation is a non-profit charity organization dedicated to connecting youth with community service opportunities, encouraging teens and international students to volunteer, and providing leadership training for teens through supporting the community.

Our Story

We began our journey as 5th graders volunteering with the Super Joey Foundation, an organization that supports kids with cancer. After being inspired by the visible difference our work made, our efforts quickly grew and we started organizing various fundraising events every year. Up till today, the funds we raised have supported over 30 events that directly benefit children with severe illnesses.

Over the years, our team has kept expanding and now includes members from 8+ schools on the East Coast. In 2023, we founded We United Foundation (WUF) with the intention of using our volunteering experience to influence and support new members who want to use their unique interests to make a positive impact. We United Foundation is not only a non-profit organization dedicated to serving kids with severe diseases, but also a community that supports youths wanting to develop themselves through community service.
