Aequum Scholars

Bio: Aequum Scholars is a 501(c)(3) non-profit founded to provide scholarships to underserved high school students interested in pursuing careers in the Sciences, Technology and Engineering fields. The founders were immigrants to the United States, and truly believe that education provides the greatest opportunity to fulfill an individual’s promise and contribute to society.

Website: https://aequumscholars.org/

Opportunities Offered:

  • Scholarship due January 31st, 2024

Stepping Stones

Bio: Stepping Stones Shelter provides emergency food and shelter to Montgomery County families experiencing homelessness. Located in the historic Dawson farmhouse in Rockville, Maryland, Stepping Stones has been serving the community for over 30 years.

Website: https://steppingstonesshelter.org/

Opportunities Offered:

  • Tutor/child care on Tuesdays and Thursdays

Butterfly Books

Bio: Butterfly Books distributes books and book-ish supplies to underserved communities through literary events, book drives, and their signature bundle donations. Butterfly Books proudly partners with their local community’s medical and care centers to install and supply Little Libraries: cabinets of books freely available for children of all ages to borrow, read, and return or keep.

Website: https://www.butterflybooks.org/

Opportunities Offered:

  • Book Drives
  • Read Alouds
